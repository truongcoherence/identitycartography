# Field Notes

The Field Notes are the raw, empirical observations that precede formal canonization. While the Active Canon represents finalized or highly stable structural mechanics, the Field Notes document the ongoing process of "seeing the terrain." They consist of real-time audits, specific case observations, and localized data points gathered during the navigation of actual professional and personal systems.
