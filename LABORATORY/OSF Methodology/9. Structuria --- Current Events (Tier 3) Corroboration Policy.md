# Structuria Current Events (Tier 3) Corroboration Policy

**Status:** July 12, 2026. Consolidates what already existed, scattered, across the Evidentiary Tier System and the retraction of the Global Structural Ingestion Protocol, into one standing policy. No new restriction is introduced here — this document exists because "the rule already exists somewhere" is not the same as "there is a policy," and the prior protocol's failure showed what happens when that distinction is ignored.

---

## 1. What Current-Event Material Is For

Generality check only. Confirms that a pattern already sourced at Tier 1 or 2 generalizes beyond the author's personal and workplace context. It may never establish a new invariant, constraint, or pattern on its own, and must always be labeled "Tier 3, generality check only, not independently weighted" wherever it appears.

## 2. Who Handles It, and How

Retrieved and pre-digested by Claude, not read raw by the author, specifically to avoid direct exposure to distressing material. Searches are filtered by structural mechanism relevant to an already-open question, not by raw topic. Graphic, violent, or purely inflammatory content is excluded where a structurally relevant summary can be extracted without it. Political material is avoided by default unless specifically requested.

## 3. When It Gets Invoked

Ad hoc, triggered by a specific, already-open analytical question — an existing Tier 1/2-sourced invariant whose generality is being checked — never on a schedule, and never as a way to generate new candidate material from scratch. If there is no existing Tier 1/2 claim already on the table, there is nothing for Tier 3 material to corroborate, and it should not be sought.

## 4. The Discipline This Exists to Protect

Tier 3 corroboration must never be used to make a thin invariant look more supported than it actually is. An invariant sourced by one Tier 2 case, with a Tier 3 generality check added, is still a single-sourced invariant — the Tier 3 material confirms the pattern isn't unique to the author, it does not add a second source in the sense the Pattern Comparison Protocol's "bar is two" requires. This should be stated plainly in provenance notes every time, not left to be inferred from the presence of a citation.

## 5. What This Policy Explicitly Does Not Permit

Everything the retracted Global Structural Ingestion Protocol proposed: AI-mediated structural extraction the author never checks against source material; global events processed on a fixed cadence independent of any open analytical question; global evidence treated as sufficient on its own to produce a "provisional law." All of this remains retracted. This document is the replacement, and it is a restriction, not a rebuild of the old protocol under a new name.
