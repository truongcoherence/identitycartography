# Structuria Laboratory

The _Laboratory_ serves as the primary integration point between the public-facing conceptual work shared on LinkedIn and the formal methodological research hosted on the Open Science Framework (OSF). This directory contains the technical artifacts, structural diagnostics, and investigative tools that constitute the "Identity Cartography" framework.

The objective of this space is transparency. It provides a structured view of the research process, allowing for the auditing of methodology and the observation of the discipline’s evolution. In the _Laboratory_, we move beyond conceptual synthesis into the mechanical reality of the systems we inhabit.
